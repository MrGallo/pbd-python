7.5. Dice¶

Write a program that simulates a dice roll by picking a random number from 1-6 and then picking a second random number from 1-6. Add the two values together, and display the total.

Name the file: dice.py

HERE COMES THE DICE!

Roll #1: 3
Roll #2: 5
The total is 8!

HERE COMES THE DICE!

Roll #1: 4
Roll #2: 2
The total is 6!
